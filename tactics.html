Formation 4-1-2-3

Defense Tactic

  • The midfield is weaker defensively compared to 4-2-3-1
  • Risky if CDM fails
  • Space between the CB and CM can lead to free space

Attack Tactic

  • Strong attacking presence with 3 forwards + 2 CMs
  • Midfielders can push forward to support attacks
  • Great for possession play and pressing high

Balanced (Attack + Defense)

This formation is more attack-oriented, but still workable defensively if the CDM is disciplined.

Best transitional tactic:

✔ CDM anchors

✔ Wingers drop quickly

✔ CMs track back in defense
